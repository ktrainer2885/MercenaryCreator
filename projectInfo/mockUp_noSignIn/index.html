The Mercenary Creator is a web application that will allow the gamemaster or the players to log on and manage their mercenary units. The mercenary creator allows the GM or players to create their characters using the Mercenary Creation Rules found in the Campaign Operations rulebook. The web application will do all the math in the background as the players create their units. At the end, they will be able to make any tweaks based on their choices or what the GM wants.

The Mercenary Creator can be yours for just $5.99 per month. The web application will kepp all of your files ready to be accessed anywhere in the world.
